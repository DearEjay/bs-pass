## 6. SAFE Conversion Example

*How the $250,000 pre-seed SAFE converts at the Seed round.*

| Variable | Value |
|---|---|
| SAFE amount | $250,000 |
| Valuation cap | $2,500,000 |
| Seed round pre-money valuation | $5,000,000 |
| Conversion valuation (lower of cap or pre-money) | $2,500,000 |
| Shares issued to SAFE investors | $250,000 ÷ ($2,500,000 ÷ 10,000,000) = 1,000,000 shares |
| SAFE investor ownership (post-seed, pre-option pool) | ~8.3% |

*SAFE investors benefit from the cap — they convert at $2.5M even if the Seed is priced at $5M. This is the return mechanism for early believers.*
