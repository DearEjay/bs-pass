# One-Pager — AI Music Management App

---

## The Problem

Independent artists are their own managers. They juggle producers, engineers, mix engineers, mastering engineers, and graphic designers across text threads, spreadsheets, and email — with no formal contracts, no clear task ownership, and no visibility into where a project actually stands. The result: tracks stall, collaborators go unpaid or underpaid, and releases get delayed or never happen.

There are 11 million independent artists globally. None of the tools they use were built for them.

---

## The Solution

An AI-powered music project management platform where an intelligent agent acts as the artist's manager — generating project roadmaps, assigning tasks by collaborator role, tracking track status from Recording to Release, and enforcing revenue splits with digital signatures.

Artists create a project, add their team, and the agent handles coordination. When a track moves to Mixing, the agent checks if a mixing engineer is assigned. If not, it creates the task. When all tracks are mastered and splits are signed, the project is marked Ready for Release.

---

## Product

- **Projects** — Album, EP, Single, Mixtape with full track lifecycle management
- **AI Manager Agent** — generates roadmaps from templates, adapts by project status, learns user preferences
- **Collaborator Management** — invite team by predefined role; all collaborators are registered users
- **Revenue Splits** — auto-populated by role, digitally signed, fully auditable
- **Real-Time Chat** — agent posts to project chat on every action; team stays aligned without Slack threads
